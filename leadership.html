Meet Our Leaders

Our forward-looking leadership team is made up of dedicated, focused and experienced executives. Working with each other and with our employees worldwide, their knowledge and experience come together to make a difference for all our stakeholders.

Rob Mallaband

Managing Director

Rob is the Managing Director of Hot Beans since January 2016. He is now leading the company through a period of dynamic transformation powered by accelerated innovation, operational excellence and strategic execution. Prior to joining us, Rob served as chairman, president and chief executive officer of Covidien plc from 2012 through 2015 and was named a director of the company in 2011. Rob previously served as president of Covidien's Medical Devices organization.

Simon Chave

Head of Recruitment

Simon has been associated with the recruitment industry for over 31 years. In his role, Simon has built up a unique insight and network into the world of recruitment, especially the companies and individuals who operate in it.​ He sees his role as actively promoting industry best practice whilst sharing, collaborating and networking with the leading recruitment companies across the globe. He is a pioneer of many sector initiatives his knowledge and recognition of the sector is universally recognised.

Natalie Moreton

Head of Finance

Natalie brings with her a wealth of experience in the development and management of financial systems and processes including reporting and analysis, budgeting and forecasting, due diligence and presentation of information to support decision making. With nearly 20 years of experience, Natalie became an associate member of CIMA in 2005 and went on to work in leading financial positions within a number of companies that served clients in a variety of sectors including energy and utilities.

Louise Clarke

Director of Client Services

Louise is responsible for strategy and delivery of Parkhouse Bells’ account managed solutions, working in partnership with our key account clients to ensure realisation and fulfilment of their strategic vision. Louise has a wealth of both recruitment and delivery experience within the skills, education and employment services sectors, working at both senior executive level and managing UK wide multi-site corporate accounts within recruitment.
